About HomeLink

We help people find homes they can trust across Zimbabwe

HomeLink is Zimbabwe's first fully verified housing marketplace. Every listing on our platform is checked by a real person before it goes live — so tenants, travellers and students can book with confidence.

Founded with a simple mission: remove the fear of scams, ghost listings and fake landlords from the housing search. We partner with local property owners, agencies and student accommodation providers to bring you homes that are exactly as described.

From city apartments in Harare to safari lodges in Victoria Falls, from student studios near UZ to family homes in Bulawayo — HomeLink connects you to verified stays, everywhere in Zimbabwe. Africa is next.
